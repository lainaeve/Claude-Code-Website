A minimalist desert escape where the landscape is the luxury. I captured the interplay of natural light, open sky, and intentional design — content built to make a viewer feel the stillness of the desert before they ever arrive.

Every frame was a study in restraint. The way Joshua trees cast long shadows at dusk. The silence that settles over sandstone at the end of the day. A space designed to disappear into its surroundings — and content made to do the same.
