About

I'm
Laina.

I'm a visual storyteller based in Washington State, creating cinematic content for luxury stays and boutique properties.

A couple of years ago, I made a decision to rebuild my life around work that actually lights me up. I traded the career that looked right on paper for the one that felt right in my soul — and I picked up a camera.

That shift changed everything. It gave me a deep appreciation for spaces that are designed with intention, for the quiet details that make a place feel like home, and for the kind of beauty that can only be captured when you slow down long enough to really see it.

I don't create content that looks like an ad. I create content that feels like a moment. I work with natural light, slow pacing, and a cinematic eye to produce reels, photography, and social content that does one thing really well: make people feel something. And when people feel something, they book.
